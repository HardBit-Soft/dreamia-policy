Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

This privacy policy describes how Dreamia ("Application", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information. By using our Application, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services.

a. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you log in to our application with your Google or Apple account, we collect the basic profile information provided by these services (such as username, email address, and profile picture).
  • Dream Content: The dream texts you submit for analysis, including the content, emotions, and any additional details you provide about your dreams.
  • Subscription Information: Information related to your premium subscription status and in-app purchases.

b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We may collect usage data such as your interactions within the application, the screens you view, the features you use, and transaction times.
  • Push Notification Token (FCM Token): To send you push notifications, we store a token generated specifically for your device.
  • Analytics Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve our app performance and user experience.
  • Crash Reports: We may collect crash reports and error logs to help us identify and fix technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide the core functionalities of the application (e.g., dream analysis, interpretation results, and personalized insights).
  • Personalization: To personalize and improve your application experience, including dream interpretation methods and content recommendations.
  • Communication: To send you service-related updates, push notifications, and respond to your support requests.
  • Subscription Management: To manage your in-app purchases and premium subscriptions.
  • Analysis and Development: To analyze the application's performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • AI Processing: To process your dream content through our AI analysis system to provide personalized interpretations and insights.
  • Content Delivery: To provide you with relevant articles, dream symbols, and educational content about dream interpretation.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties without your consent. However, we may exchange information with the following services to provide our services:

  • Google Services: For authentication, analytics, and cloud storage services.
  • Apple Services: For authentication and subscription management.
  • Firebase/Google Cloud: For data storage, authentication, and app functionality.
  • Adapty: For subscription management and payment processing.
  • AI Services: Your dream content may be processed by third-party AI services to provide dream analysis and interpretation.

4. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard technical and administrative security measures to ensure the security of your data. Your dream content and personal information are encrypted and stored securely using Firebase security measures. Your data is retained for as long as necessary to provide our services or to comply with our legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have various rights over your data. You can access and update the personal information associated with your account or request to delete your account. Upon an account deletion request, your data will be permanently removed from our systems, including all dream analyses and personal content.

  • Data Access: You can view and download your dream analyses and personal data.
  • Data Correction: You can update your profile information and dream content.
  • Data Deletion: You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage your premium subscription through your device's app store settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will post the new policy in the application and update the "Last Updated" date. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email.
